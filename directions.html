How to Come to Dr. Allyn's Office

Beginning at the intersection of Shea and Fountain Hills Boulevards:

Go north on Fountain Hills Boulevard approximately 1.5 miles.

Turn right onto Inca Avenue (4-way stop).

Proceed to 16425 East Inca Avenue.

Turn right into the drive way.

Park your car near the front entrance.

Ring the bell labeled "Doctor's Office" and I will be there to greet you.
